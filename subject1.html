  • Summary

    • The disastrous life of Saiki K. follows a boy named Saiki Kusuo as he tries to hide his psychic abilities in a completely normal school. The show takes place in PK academy during his sophomore year of high school. Saiki was born with all kinds of abnormal psychic abilities, like Pyrokinesis and teleportation. Some key characteristics are his vibrant pink hair, tinted lime glasses, and bubblegum pink antenna hair clips. Saiki was born with vibrant pink hair while the entire world was born with normal hair, so using his mind control he made everybody have vibrant colorful hair. Saiki wears tinted green glasses because he can turn people into stone if he looks at them with his bare eyes. His two hair clip antennas suppress most of his powers. In the beginning of the story Saiki is very closed off and has no friends. But as the story continues he gains two new friends Riki Nendo and Shun Kaido. The anime and manga progresses as Saiki and his friends are put into normal school scenarios in which he solves them with psychic abilities.
